Six Hours of Silence

Behavioral Flow, Not Forecast

For six hours, the market moved less than 0.1%. No spikes, no volume bursts, no visible catalysts. This isn’t randomness — it’s restraint.

Silence often precedes structure. When participants stop reacting, they start observing. This phase is not passive — it’s preparatory.

We document these windows not to predict, but to recognize repetition. Silence is a signal when it repeats across timeframes and assets.
